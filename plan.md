# The Direct-to-Owner Automation Agency (2026)

## 🎯 The Vision
Skip the middleman. Instead of selling leads to agencies, you solve expensive problems for business owners directly. You use your engineering skills to automate **Service Audits** that find broken things on their websites, and then you offer an automated fix for a monthly subscription.
